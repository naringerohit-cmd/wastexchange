⚠ Why EPR compliance matters

Under India's Plastic Waste Management Rules 2022, E-Waste Rules 2022, and Battery Waste Rules 2022, producers and importers must meet EPR targets. Proper waste documentation protects your factory from CPCB/GPCB penalties. Every transaction through our platform generates a verified EPR compliance certificate.
